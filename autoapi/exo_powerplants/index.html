exo_powerplants

Created on Fri Nov 25 13:33:33 2022

@author: Mathias Berg Rosendal, Research Assistant DTU Management

Reads the Danish Energy Agency’s “Energiproducenttælling” (EPT, includes solar power) and windturbines datasheet.

It is converted and aggregated to a GKFX.inc file for Balmorel.

Download EPT and datasheet for windturbines here: https://ens.dk/service/statistik-data-noegletal-og-kort/data-oversigt-over-energisektoren
